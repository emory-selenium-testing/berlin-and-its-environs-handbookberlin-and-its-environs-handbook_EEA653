{% raw %}Baths. 31{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Military Reviews ('Paraden'). A magnificent military spec¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tacle is afforded every year, at the end of May and beginning of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}September, by the review of the various regiments of Guards held{% endraw %}
{% raw %}by the Emperor on the Tempelhofer Feld (p. 173). Admission to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the tribunes (6000 seats; tickets at the Invalidendank, p. 27), 3 -{% endraw %}
{% raw %}10^; programme 20 pf. For carriages a special permit from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'Polizei-Prasidium' is necessary, for which visitors should apply in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}good time; if no answer is received, the applicant must understand{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that all space available has already been assigned. The troops,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with the Emperor at the head, march back through the Belle-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Alliance-Strasse.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Guard Mounting at the Royal Guard House (p. 59) takes place{% endraw %}
{% raw %}daily at 12.45 p.m. (during the manoeuvres, at 2.45 p.m.), after{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which, when the weather is good, the band plays in the Lustgarten,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}near the statue of Frederick William III. (p. 63).{% endraw %}
