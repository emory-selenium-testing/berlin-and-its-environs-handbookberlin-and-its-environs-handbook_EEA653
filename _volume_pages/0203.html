{% raw %}The Zoological Museum is an admirably selected collection,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}illustrating all the important groups of the animal world, with the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fauna of Germany placed in the foreground (official guide 20 pf.).{% endraw %}
