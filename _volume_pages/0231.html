{% raw %}14. Potsdam and its Environs.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A visit to Potsdam is highly recommended on account both of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}its natural beauties and its historical associations. The spacious{% endraw %}
{% raw %}parks with their rivers and fountains and the palaces with their{% endraw %}
{% raw %}well-preserved interiors and contemporary decorations and furniture{% endraw %}
{% raw %}present us with a better picture of a royal residence of the 18th{% endraw %}
{% raw %}century than can be seen even at the larger and more pretentious{% endraw %}
{% raw %}palace of Versailles, which was deprived of so much of its splendour{% endraw %}
{% raw %}by the Revolution. If Versailles has the advantages of a uniform{% endraw %}
{% raw %}scheme in its arrangement, Potsdam has the charm of variety.{% endraw %}
