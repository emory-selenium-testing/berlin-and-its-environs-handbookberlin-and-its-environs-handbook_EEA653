{% raw %}72 Section 3. BERLIN. Old Museum:{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Upper Vestibule, to which a double staircase ascends, is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}adorned with a copy of the celebrated antique Warwick Vase in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}England. The frescoes, also designed by Schinkel, represent the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}struggle of civilised mankind against barbarians and the elements.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A fine view of the Lustgarten with its fountains, the cathedral,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Schloss, etc., is obtained hence from between the columns.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Old Museum contains exclusively antique works of art and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}examples of applied art dating from the Grseco-Roman period.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Roman Empire and freely restored, but the purchase of the Sahu-{% endraw %}

{% raw %}have placed the gallery upon quite a different footing. The sculp¬{% endraw %}
